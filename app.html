🧮 How E-Pricing Works

Understanding our Smart Score algorithm

The E-Pricing Smart Score Formula

SmartScore = (Price × 0.35) + (Quality × 0.25) + (Stock × 0.20) + (Trust × 0.20)
💰

Price Score (35% weight)

Measures how cheap this listing is compared to others.

PriceScore = ((MaxPrice − SitePrice) ÷ (MaxPrice − MinPrice)) × 100

The cheapest site gets 100, the most expensive gets 0.

⭐

Quality Score (25% weight)

Based on user ratings out of 5 stars.

QualityScore = (Rating ÷ 5) × 100

A 4.5★ rated product scores 90 out of 100.

📦

Stock Score (20% weight)

Checks if the product is currently available to buy.

StockScore = In Stock ? 100 : 0

Out of stock = 0 points. This heavily penalizes unavailable listings.

🛡️

Trust Score (20% weight)

Combines seller verification and review volume.

TrustScore = (Verified ? 50 : 0) + min(ReviewCount ÷ 200, 50)

A verified seller with 10,000+ reviews gets full 100 trust points.

🏆 How the Best Deal is Chosen

The platform with the highest Smart Score is recommended as the best deal. This ensures the recommendation isn't just the cheapest option — it considers whether the product is in stock, sold by a trusted seller, and well-rated by real customers. A ₹500 savings means nothing if the seller is unverified or the item is out of stock.
